Playlists

A collection of Spotify and Deezer playlists created by the month. I started this project in mid-2015 as a single zeitgeist playlist, but in March 2016 I decided to make monthly playlists to see how my taste in music changes on a monthly basis (it doesn't really change that much, apparently). In 2019, I started doing seasonal playlists (based on the southern hemisphere) instead of monthly playlists.
