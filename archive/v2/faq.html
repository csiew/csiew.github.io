What do I do?

I'm a software engineering student at Monash University. I like to do my own projects based on what I learn at uni... sometimes things I'm learning by myself outside of uni too.

What got you interested in computers?

It all started when I walked into an Apple store in 2007. At the time my family was using one of those generic PCs with a Pentium 4 CPU, running Windows XP. That shit was slow. Scrubbing through a video clip in iMovie on an iMac was like trying frozen yogurt for the first time (i.e. why aren't all computers like this?).

Why did you develop yet another Linux distribution?

Because it's fun (and educational)! I'm doing it to figure out how the whole shebang works - from the Linux filesystem to how packages are updated.

Why does this site look so awful?

I have my reasons. Real evidence of my web design skills could be seen on Antorca's project site.
